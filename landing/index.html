Concept

The Labyrinth = Memory. Paths appear and fade, like recollection. Stabilize memories with short, soothing puzzles and light the way out. Each cleared level restores a star to a personal constellation — the moments we choose to keep.

Why it matters

Starmaze celebrates memory without medicalizing it. It’s about connection: partners, friends, and chosen family — across generations. Designed for accessibility: large text, high contrast, simple inputs, optional hints, and audio cues.

Gameplay

Explore

Navigate a serene maze that softly shifts over time. Keyboard arrows, WASD, or swipe/tap.

Recall

Short memory puzzles: match pairs, repeat colors, order story frames. No fail state; gentle retries.

Shine

Each solved memory lights a beacon, pushing back the Forgetfulness — an abstract, friendly shadow.

Values & Inclusion

Stories feature LGBTQ+ characters across life stages — tenderness, humor, everyday courage. We partner with community voices and keep representation authentic and joyful.
